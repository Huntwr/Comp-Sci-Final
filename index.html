Data Tools

        Computers are incredible with numbers and storing data. They revolutionized the way that we store and analyze data. A common way that computers store data is by using plain text files, they are small and easy to organize. You can also organize the data into CSV, comma-separated values. This helps organize the data and is useful for breaking down what the data is and analyzing it. Databases are the most common way to store data, they hold data in different files and formats and allow users to analyze or extract the specific data they need without taking excess data. Databases are the best way of storing for the amount of storage and the tools that come with it.

Big data is extremely helpful to understanding the world around us and how people use the internet but is also extremely difficult to store due to the massive size and amount of data.

Bias in Machine Learning

        Machine learning is an extremely important and extremely popular subject in the computer science space right now, it is a way of using algorithms to make a computer “think” for itself. Of course, it's not thinking for itself it is simply following a set of rules to get a specific outcome. Computer scientists can feed computers inputs and expected outputs to train the algorithms, Although because of human bias with the information being fed to the computers it can create bias for the computer. In 2016 an algorithm was being tested that would judge how likely criminals were to reoffend. This algorithm overestimated the reoffending rate of black defendants and underestimated the white defendants. The algorithm was not trained to know the race of the defendants yet it was able to sort based on race. Algorithms are trained purely off of what humans feed them therefore human bias easily sneaks its way into the output of the algorithm.

I am going to use the data to determine if the length of a movie on Netflix has any correlation with the rating it received (G,PG,PG-13,R)

I will be using a database that has records of movies and TV shows on Netflix as of mid-2021, I will be investigating if the length of movies on Netflix has any correlation to the movie's MPA Rating system (Motion Picture Association Film Rating System). For the purposes of this research I will be referring to the movie's MPA Rating just as its rating, note this has nothing to do with the quality of the movie but the material's suitability for different age groups.

Conclusion

In conclusion, the length of the film has nothing to do with its rating. Through my research, I discovered that R-rated movies are the most common on Netflix with most of the R rated movies being 94 or 97 minutes long. But after looking at the average length of each of the ratings it can be seen that the higher the rating the longer the movie but this is not completely accurate. PG-13 has on average the longest movies but PG-13 is not the highest rating a movie can receive NR (Non-Rated) which can have any type of content and could be higher than R in some situations only has an average run time of 94 minutes.
